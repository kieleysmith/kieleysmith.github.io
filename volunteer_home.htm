Welcome to the SPCA of Northern Nevada

Rescue, Adopt, For Life

The SPCA, or Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals, is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving as a shelter for homeless animals, and caring for them until they can find a new home through adoption. The SPCA of Northern Nevada is particularly focused on providing veteranary care and spay/neuter services for dogs and cats in the Reno area.

A History of Helping

The SPCA of Northern Nevada's dedication to animal well-being has been persisting since 1998, when the site was founded by a volunteer workforce. We are still heavily reliant upon community involvement and volunteerism, and look forward to cooperating with Reno citizens to improve the lives of disadvantaged animals for many more years.
