Adoptions

Adoption Fees

Dog:
Puppy (-6 mo.) : $200
Adult (+6 mo.) : $75
Senior (+7 yr.) : $40

Cat:
Puppy (-6 mo.) : $75
Adult (+6 mo.) : $30
Senior (+7 yr.) : $20

Adoption Rules

Those wishing to adopt an animal from the SPCA must be 21 years of age or older, live in an environment that allows animals, be able to provide the proper veteranary care for the animal, and promise to keep all cats contained within the house. In addition to this, the applicant must pay the adoption fee indicated above.
